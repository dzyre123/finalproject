About me

My name is D'Zyre Jones. I am a junior Computer Information Systems major, Graphic Design and Aerospace double minor from Syracuse, New York. I chose Howard because I thought it would be instrumental in the education of my people. I wanted to familiarize myself with my roots even more so than the ones of where I come from -- to learn about the brother, but most importantly to be able to look in the mirror and create a relationship of understanding with the young woman looking back at me. I'm the second oldest to four kids, middle child if I include my non-blood siblings, and the first one to go to college.
